Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026  |  Last Updated: May 2026

WorthDaGive Wellness Studio is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights as a data subject.

1. Responsible Party

WorthDaGive Wellness Studio is the responsible party (data controller) under POPIA.

  • Address: Shop 9 Constantia Square, Corner Jim Fouche & Antelope Turn, Constantia Kloof, Johannesburg, 1709
  • Email: info@worthdagive.co.za

2. Personal Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Registration: Full name, email address, date of birth, WhatsApp/mobile number (optional), password (stored hashed — never in plain text).
  • Orders & Checkout: Name, email, phone number, delivery address, order history, payment method (card details are processed by YOCO and never stored by us).
  • Reviews: Name (as provided) and review text submitted via the site.
  • Newsletter: Email address and name provided when subscribing.
  • Support/Helpline: Message content and contact details submitted via support tickets.
  • WhatsApp Ordering: WhatsApp phone number, order details communicated via WhatsApp Business.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Page View Analytics: Pages visited and timestamps, collected via our own Supabase database. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking cookies. No personal identifiers are stored in analytics records.
  • Age Verification: We store a browser flag (localStorage) indicating you have verified your age. No biometric data is collected.
  • Cookies: We use a minimal cookie consent cookie and session cookies required for account login. See our cookie notice for details.

3. How We Use Your Personal Information

We process your personal information only for the following lawful purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders, including delivery and payment confirmation.
  • Creating and managing your account.
  • Sending transactional emails: order confirmations, shipping updates, password resets.
  • Sending newsletter/marketing emails where you have opted in (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • Verifying that you are 18 years of age or older as required by law.
  • Complying with our legal obligations under the Cannabis for Private Purposes Act 7 of 2024, SAHPRA regulations, and SAPS requirements.
  • Improving our website and services through anonymised analytics.
  • Responding to your support queries.
  • Fraud prevention and security.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the following bases under POPIA:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil your order.
  • Legal Obligation: Age verification, cannabis regulatory compliance.
  • Consent: Newsletter subscriptions, optional WhatsApp number for order updates.
  • Legitimate Interest: Fraud prevention, site analytics (using anonymised data).

5. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We share your data only with:

  • Supabase (supabase.com): Our database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (AWS eu-west-1 or nearest region). Supabase is GDPR-compliant and we have a data processing agreement in place.
  • YOCO: Payment processor. Card details are handled entirely by YOCO's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.
  • Delivery Partners: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with courier partners solely for delivery purposes.
  • Make.com (Integromat): Used for WhatsApp Business automation. Order confirmation data is processed through Make.com webhooks for notification purposes only.
  • Cloudinary: Product images are hosted on Cloudinary's CDN.
  • Law Enforcement: We may disclose personal information to SAPS or other authorities when required by law or court order.

6. Data Retention

  • Order records: Retained for 5 years for tax and legal compliance.
  • Account data: Retained for the lifetime of your account. You may request deletion at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Analytics: Anonymised page view data retained for 90 days.
  • Newsletter: Retained until you unsubscribe.

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information we hold.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing.
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa: justice.gov.za/inforeg/

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@worthdagive.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies on our Supabase database.
  • Password hashing (bcrypt via Supabase Auth).
  • Regular review of access controls.

No system is completely secure. If you suspect a data breach affecting your account, please contact us immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via a notice on the website. Continued use of the website after changes constitute acknowledgement of the updated policy.
